👨‍🏫 About Me

Xiaokang Jin is currently pursuing his Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Technology at Zhejiang Normal University. He received his B.S. and M.S. degrees from Changsha University of Science and Technology in 2016 and 2019, respectively. He is currently a lecturer at Jinhua Advanced Research Institute, China. He has published more than 10 research papers in peer-reviewed journals and conferences.
