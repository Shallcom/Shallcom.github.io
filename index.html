I am currently pursuing my PhD degree in Computer Science and Technology from Zhejiang Normal University, under the supervision of Prof. Jia Zhu. Previously, I obtained my B.S. and M.S. degrees in Computer Science and Technology from Changsha University of Science and Technology, advised by Prof. Jianming Zhang.

I am also currently serving as a Lecturer at Jinhua Advanced Research Institute. My research interests focus on Computer Vision and Multimodal AI, with a particular emphasis on Educational Video Understanding, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), Object Tracking, and Knowledge Graphs.
