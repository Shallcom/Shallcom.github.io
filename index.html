    Xiaokang Jin

    Jinhua Advanced Research Institute

    • Lecturer
    • Jinhua, 321013, China
    • Email
    • ORCID

    Xiaokang Jin received the B.S. degree and the M.S. degree from Changsha University of Science and Technology in 2016 and 2019 respectively. He is currently a lecturer from Jinhua Advanced Research Institute, China. He has published more than 10 research papers. His research interests include computer vision, pattern recognition and high-performance computing.
